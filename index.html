Event at a Glance

What it is: Shark Tank is a student‑run pitching contest where teams present business ideas to a panel of judges and investors as part of TechFest GLAU 2K26.

Who participates: College students from GLA University and nearby colleges, pitching in teams through a 5–7 minute presentation followed by a short Q&A.

Date: 30th January 2026

Time: 10:30 AM onwards

Venue: Room 1043, AB‑12, GLA University

Expected Footfall: Approximately 250 students, faculty, and visitors.

Why Sponsor Shark Tank?

  • Reach young, motivated students who are future customers, hires, and potential interns.
  • Build strong, positive brand visibility at a key TechFest flagship event.
  • Support entrepreneurship, innovation, and local startup culture in and around Mathura.

Event Highlights

  • Structured 5–7 minute startup pitches followed by Q&A with judges.
  • Stage presence and branding opportunities for partner companies.
  • Mix of technology, management, and creative ideas from diverse student teams.

Sponsorship Options

A. Goodies / Hampers (Preferred)

(worth atleast RS 10000) Branded notebooks, pens, water bottles, T‑shirts, tech accessories, gift vouchers, snacks, or sample products for participants and winners.

B. Cash Sponsorship

Minimum requested contribution: ₹10,000. Higher amounts are welcome and can be tied to enhanced visibility and branding.

C. Hybrid / Other Support

(RS 5000 + RS 5000)Combination of goodies and cash, discount codes, gift vouchers, or in‑kind support such as printing, banners, and stage setup.

Custom partnership ideas are welcome and will be discussed and recorded in writing.

What Sponsors Receive

  • Logo display on event banners, standees, and relevant digital creatives at the venue.
  • Public acknowledgement and thanks during the event by the emcee or coordinator.
  • Esteemed Investors and Sponsors are Cordially invited as VIPs
  • Opportunity to keep promotional leaflets, coupons, QR codes, or product samples at the registration desk; stall space can be arranged on request.
  • Formal Certificate of Appreciation and a simple signed receipt after fulfilment.

Final placement, size, and number of mentions will depend on the sponsorship type and amount, and will be clearly mentioned in the written confirmation document.

Important Terms

  • All sponsorships must be confirmed through written communication; no verbal commitments will be treated as final.
  • Communication should be via the coordinator’s official GLA email ID.
  • Goodies / hampers should reach the venue or specified address at least 2 days before the event, or as mutually agreed in writing.
  • Cash sponsorship must be transferred to the specified account or paid in cash/cheque before the event as per the written agreement.
  • Sponsors will receive a signed receipt and Certificate of Appreciation from the organising team.

How to Confirm Your Sponsorship

  1. Choose your preferred option: Goodies / Cash / Hybrid.
  2. Email the coordinator with company name, contact person, phone number, email, chosen option, and approximate quantity or amount.
  3. Review and approve the short sponsorship confirmation document sent to you.
  4. Send the goodies or complete the payment as per the agreed date and mode.

A simple one‑page agreement can be provided if your organisation prefers a formal contract.
